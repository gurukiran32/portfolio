About Me

Hi, I'm Gurukiran Veerapur

I’m an Associate SecOps Engineer at o9 Solutions based in Bengaluru, India. I’m passionate about bridging the gap between software development and cybersecurity operations, ensuring that applications are not only efficient but also secure and resilient.

Outside of work, I love exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, and expanding my knowledge in AI, Security, and Cloud Computing.
